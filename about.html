About Our Ministry

Transforming lives through the power of God's Word

Our Story

The Sons of God Ministry was founded in 1995 with a vision to create a place where people from all walks of life could experience God's love and grow in their faith journey. What began as a small Bible study in the living room of Pastor Johnson has grown into a thriving community of believers dedicated to sharing the Gospel and making a difference in Pittsburgh and beyond.

Through the years, we've remained committed to our founding principles of authentic worship, Biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach. As we've grown, we've expanded our ministries to meet the needs of our community while staying true to our mission of helping people know God, find freedom, discover purpose, and make a difference.

Today, The Sons of God Ministry continues to be a place where lives are transformed through the power of God's Word and the love of His people.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ as the Son of God who died for our sins and rose again, offering salvation to all who believe in Him.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit who empowers believers to live godly lives and equips them with gifts for ministry.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, grow in faith, and share the Gospel with the world.

Service

We believe in serving others as Christ served us, meeting both spiritual and practical needs in our community.

Missions

We believe in the Great Commission and are committed to sharing the Gospel locally and around the world.
