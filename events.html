Community Outreach

9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Meet at Church Campus

Join us as we serve our local community through various outreach projects. We'll be partnering with local organizations to provide food, clothing, and other essential items to those in need. This is a great opportunity to make a tangible difference in our community and show God's love in practical ways.

What to Bring

  • Work gloves
  • Water bottle
  • Comfortable clothes
  • A heart to serve
