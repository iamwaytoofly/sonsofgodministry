About Our Ministry

The Sons of God Ministry is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community where people from all walks of life come together to experience God's love and grow in their faith journey.

We believe in creating a space where everyone can have authentic encounters with Christ, discover their gifts, and use them to impact our community and the world.
