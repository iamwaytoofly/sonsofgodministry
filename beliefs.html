Statement of Faith

Rooted in Scripture. Centered on Christ. Guided by the Spirit.

What We Believe

1. The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is the final authority for faith and life (2 Timothy 3:16-17; 2 Peter 1:20-21).

2. The Trinity

We believe in one eternal God, existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—coequal and coeternal (Genesis 1:1, 26; Matthew 28:19; 2 Corinthians 13:14).

3. God the Father

We believe God the Father is the Creator of all things, perfect in holiness, wisdom, power, and love. He concerns Himself mercifully in the affairs of men (Psalm 145:8-9; 1 Corinthians 8:6).

4. Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God. He was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the virgin Mary, lived a sinless life, performed miracles, died on the cross for our sins, rose bodily from the dead, ascended into heaven, and will return personally in power and glory (Matthew 1:18-25; John 1:1-4; 1 Corinthians 15:3-4).

5. The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit glorifies Jesus Christ, convicts people of sin, regenerates the believing sinner, and indwells, guides, instructs, and empowers the believer for godly living and service (John 16:7-14; Acts 1:8; Romans 8:9-17).

6. Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift of God based on the redemptive work of Jesus Christ. It is received by grace through faith alone. Repentance and faith in Jesus Christ result in regeneration by the Holy Spirit (Ephesians 2:8-9; John 3:16-17; Titus 3:4-7).

7. The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, composed of all true believers. Its purpose is to worship God, grow in faith, serve others, and share the Gospel with the world (Ephesians 1:22-23; Acts 2:42-47; Matthew 28:18-20).

8. Ordinances

We believe the Lord Jesus Christ instituted two ordinances for the Church: water baptism and the Lord’s Supper. Both are symbolic and point to the believer’s faith in Christ (Matthew 28:19; 1 Corinthians 11:23-26).

9. Christian Living

We believe every Christian is called to live a life set apart for God, empowered by the Holy Spirit, demonstrating love, integrity, and service (Romans 12:1-2; Galatians 5:22-23).

10. The Return of Christ

We believe in the personal and imminent return of Jesus Christ to judge the living and the dead, and to establish His eternal kingdom (Acts 1:11; 1 Thessalonians 4:13-18; Revelation 22:12-13).

11. Eternity

We believe in the bodily resurrection of all people: the saved to eternal life with God, and the lost to eternal separation from God (John 5:28-29; Revelation 20:11-15).
