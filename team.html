Meet Our Team

The dedicated servants who lead and support our ministry

Senior Leadership

Pastor Rose Nance

Lead Pastor

Pastor Rose has been leading The Sons of God Ministry since its founding in 2004. With over 30 years of ministry experience, she is passionate about teaching the Word of God and helping people discover their purpose in Christ. Her vision for the church is to create a community where everyone can experience God's love and grow in their faith journey.

Pastor Michael Reynolds

Associate Pastor

Pastor Michael serves as our Associate Pastor, working closely with Pastor Rose to shepherd our congregation. With a background in counseling and a heart for discipleship, he oversees our small groups ministry and provides pastoral care. His gentle approach and wisdom have helped countless members navigate life's challenges through a biblical perspective.

Jennifer Williams

Executive Director

Jennifer oversees the administrative and operational aspects of our ministry. With a background in business management and a heart for service, she ensures that our church runs smoothly so that our pastoral team can focus on spiritual leadership. Her organizational skills and strategic thinking have been instrumental in our church's growth and development.

Worship Ministry

Sarah Johnson

Worship Pastor

Sarah leads our worship ministry with a heart for creating authentic worship experiences that draw people into God's presence. She has been serving at The Sons of God Ministry for over 10 years and has a gift for developing worship leaders and musicians. Her passion is to see people encounter God through worship and experience transformation.

Marcus Davis

Music Director

Marcus brings over 15 years of musical experience to our worship team. With a background in gospel music and formal training in music theory, he works with our musicians to create a sound that is both excellent and anointed. His arrangements and original compositions have become beloved parts of our worship experience.

Alicia Martinez

Choir Director

Alicia leads our church choir with passion and excellence. Her background in vocal performance and choral direction has elevated our choir ministry to new heights. She believes in the power of corporate worship and works tirelessly to train and develop the vocal gifts within our congregation.

Youth & Children's Ministry

David Thompson

Youth Pastor

David has a passion for helping young people grow in their faith and discover their God-given potential. He has been leading our youth ministry for 8 years and has developed innovative programs that engage teens in meaningful ways. His approach combines biblical teaching with relevant application to the challenges young people face today.

Rebecca Wilson

Children's Ministry Director

Rebecca leads our children's ministry with creativity and enthusiasm. With a background in early childhood education, she creates age-appropriate programs that make biblical truths accessible and exciting for children. Her team of dedicated volunteers provides a safe, nurturing environment where children can learn about God's love.

Jason Rodriguez

College Ministry Coordinator

Jason bridges the gap between our youth and adult ministries by leading our college and young adult programs. His relatable teaching style and genuine approach to mentorship have created a thriving community for those navigating the critical years of early adulthood. He organizes weekly gatherings, retreats, and service opportunities.

Outreach & Missions

James Wilson

Outreach Director

James leads our community outreach initiatives with a heart for serving the Pittsburgh area. His background in social work and his passion for justice drive our ministry's efforts to address practical needs in our community. Under his leadership, we've developed partnerships with local organizations to maximize our impact.

Grace Chen

Missions Coordinator

Grace coordinates our global missions efforts, maintaining relationships with our missionary partners around the world. Having served as a missionary herself for 5 years, she brings valuable experience and cultural sensitivity to our missions program. She organizes our short-term mission trips and keeps our congregation connected to our global impact.

Robert Jackson

Community Service Coordinator

Robert organizes our local service projects and volunteer opportunities. His background in nonprofit management helps him identify meaningful ways for our congregation to serve the community. From food drives to home repairs for the elderly, he connects our members' skills with community needs.

Administration & Support

Patricia Adams

Office Manager

Patricia keeps our church operations running smoothly with her exceptional organizational skills. She manages our church calendar, coordinates facility use, and serves as the first point of contact for many visitors. Her warm personality and efficiency make her an invaluable part of our administrative team.

Thomas Lee

Media & Technology Director

Thomas oversees our media ministry, including our live streaming, sound system, and website. With a background in IT and a passion for using technology to spread the gospel, he ensures that our message reaches beyond our physical walls. His team of volunteers works behind the scenes to create a seamless worship experience.

Daniel Brown

Facilities Manager

Daniel ensures that our church building is well-maintained and ready for all ministry activities. His attention to detail and proactive approach keep our facilities in excellent condition. From coordinating repairs to overseeing our custodial team, he creates a welcoming environment for worship and fellowship.
