Pastor Ramon Nance – Assistant Pastor

Pastor Ramon Nance serves as an Assistant Pastor at The Sons of God Ministry, where his primary focus is on outreach — connecting the church with the wider community and sharing the hope of Christ beyond the walls of the sanctuary. Compassionate and steadfast in his belief in the Bible and in his faith, Pastor Ramon leads teams and initiatives that bring practical help and spiritual encouragement to those in need.

With a heart for evangelism and community partnerships, he creates opportunities for people from all walks of life to experience God’s love in action. Pastor Ramon also supports the Senior Pastor in teaching, pastoral care, and ministry planning, ensuring that outreach remains at the heart of the church’s mission.

He is committed to seeing lives transformed and neighborhoods strengthened through the power of the gospel.

Pastor Ron Nance II – Assistant Pastor

Pastor Ronald Nance II serves as an Assistant Pastor at The Sons of God Ministry, where his primary focus is on administrative leadership and young adult ministry. With a true heart for ministry, he combines strong organizational skills with a passion for helping members grow in faith and purpose.

Pastor Ronald is dedicated to creating environments and opportunities where people can develop meaningful, personal relationships with Jesus Christ. In addition to overseeing church operations and supporting the Senior Pastor, he mentors emerging leaders and equips the next generation to live out their faith boldly.

His commitment to excellence and spiritual growth helps ensure that every area of The Sons of God Ministry runs smoothly while staying centered on the gospel.
