Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

There is no dress code at The Sons of God Ministry, Inc. Some people dress casually in jeans and t-shirts, while others prefer to wear more formal attire. We want you to feel comfortable, so come as you are!

Where do I park?

We have a large parking lot with designated visitor parking spaces near the main entrance. Look for the "First-Time Visitor" signs. If our lot is full, additional parking is available across the street at the community center on Sundays.

What about my children?

We offer age-appropriate children's ministry programs during all of our services for infants through 5th grade. Our children's area is secure, and all volunteers undergo background checks. When you arrive, visit our children's check-in area, and our team will help get your children registered.

How long are the services?

Our services typically last about 75 minutes. They include worship through music, announcements about church events, and a message from God's Word.

Will I be asked to give money?

As a guest, you are not expected to give. We do receive an offering during our services as part of our worship, but this is for our regular attendees. Feel free to let the offering plate pass by when it comes to you.

Do you have programs for teenagers?

Yes! Our youth ministry serves students in 6th-12th grade and meets on Wednesday nights at 7:00 PM. Teens are also encouraged to attend our regular Sunday services with their families.
