Plan Your Visit

We're excited to welcome you! Here’s everything you need to know before you arrive.

What to Expect

  • Warm and friendly greeting at the door.
  • Free parking available on site.
  • Casual and comfortable atmosphere—come as you are.
  • Uplifting worship music and inspiring message.
  • Kids' ministry and safe, engaging programs for children.
  • Service lasts about 90 minutes.
  • Coffee and snacks available before and after service.
  • Opportunities to connect and ask questions afterwards.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early to find parking, check in your kids, and get settled.

Is there a dress code?

Nope! Dress comfortably. Most people wear casual clothes.

Is childcare provided?

Yes! We offer safe, fun programs for kids of all ages during all services.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself! We provide everything you’ll need during service.

Do I have to participate?

No pressure—you’re welcome to simply observe and participate as you feel comfortable.

How long is the service?

Services typically last about 90 minutes.

Can I attend if I’m not religious?

Absolutely—you’re welcome no matter your background or beliefs.
