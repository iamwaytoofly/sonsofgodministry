Finding Strength in Weakness

"But he said to me, 'My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.' Therefore I will boast all the more gladly about my weaknesses, so that Christ's power may rest on me." - 2 Corinthians 12:9

In a world that celebrates strength and self-sufficiency, God's economy works differently. Paul learned that his weakness was actually the conduit through which God's power could flow most effectively. When we acknowledge our limitations and depend fully on God, we position ourselves to experience His supernatural strength.

Today, instead of hiding your weaknesses or feeling ashamed of them, bring them before God. Surrender your inadequacies to Him and watch how His power works through your vulnerability.

Prayer

Lord, I acknowledge my weaknesses before You today. Thank You that Your strength is made perfect in my weakness. Help me to depend on You fully and to see my limitations as opportunities for Your power to be displayed. In Jesus' name, Amen.
