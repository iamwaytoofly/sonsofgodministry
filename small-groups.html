Why Join a Small Group?

At The Sons of God Ministry, we believe that spiritual growth happens best in community. Small groups provide a place where you can:

  • Connect with others and build meaningful relationships
  • Grow in your understanding of God's Word
  • Find support and encouragement in your faith journey
  • Discover and use your spiritual gifts
  • Experience authentic community and belonging

Our small groups meet throughout the week in various locations, including homes, coffee shops, and at our church campus. Most groups meet for 8-12 weeks per semester, with breaks between semesters.

Lead a Small Group

Do you have a passion for building community and helping others grow in their faith? Consider leading a small group!

As a small group leader, you'll:

  • Facilitate meaningful discussions
  • Create a welcoming environment for group members
  • Receive training and ongoing support
  • Make a significant impact in people's lives

No experience necessary - we provide all the training and resources you need to lead effectively.
