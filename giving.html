Why We Give

At The Sons of God Ministry, Inc., we believe that giving is an act of worship. When we give, we acknowledge that everything we have comes from God, and we express our gratitude by giving back a portion of what He has given us.

Your generosity helps us:

  • Support our local community through outreach programs
  • Fund mission trips and global initiatives
  • Maintain our facilities for worship and fellowship
  • Provide resources for children, youth, and adult ministries
  • Assist those in need through our benevolence fund

Thank you for partnering with us in ministry through your faithful giving.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." - 2 Corinthians 9:7
